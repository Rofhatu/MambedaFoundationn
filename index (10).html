About Our Foundation

Creating pathways from adversity to achievement

Our Mission

The Mambeda Foundation empowers individuals to unlock their potential through access to education and employment, while providing a foundation of care and support for orphaned or underprivileged children to build a brighter future.

Our Vision

A world where every person, regardless of background, has the guidance to succeed and where every orphaned or underprivileged child grows up in a supportive environment with equal opportunities.
